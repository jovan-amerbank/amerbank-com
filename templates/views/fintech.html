Fintech

Amerbank relies on two core systems for payment processing:

Titan

Amerbank’s in-house solution for transaction monitoring or our AML software. Titan can identify and prevent potential financial crimes, as well as ensure that payments are processed thoroughly, efficiently and timely manner. It helps facilitate safe and secure transactions and mitigate regulatory pressures of international banking.

Kronos

Amebank’s netbanking platform that enables our clients financial to initiate new transactions, view incoming payments, manage users and generate account statements. It is also used for payment status monitoring, sharing KYCC data and tracking account balance.
